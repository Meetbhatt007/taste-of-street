Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Taste of Street?
    Taste of Street is a platform that helps you discover, locate, and enjoy the best street food vendors and trending foods in your city. We connect food lovers with authentic local flavors and experiences.
  • How do I find street food vendors near me?
    Use our interactive map and smart filters on the homepage to search for vendors by location, food type, distance, and rating. You can also explore top-rated and trending stalls in your area.
  • Is there a subscription or membership?
    Yes! You can subscribe for exclusive access to food trails, events, and special offers. New members get a 1-month free trial and discounts on their first month.
  • How can I review a vendor or food stall?
    After visiting a vendor, log in to your account and leave a review on their profile page. Your feedback helps others discover great food and supports local vendors.
  • Can I add my own street food stall to Taste of Street?
    Absolutely! If you are a vendor, you can register your stall through our Vendors page. Join our community and reach more food lovers in your city.
  • How do I contact support?
    You can reach us anytime at info@tasteofstreet.com or use the Live Chat option on the website for quick assistance.
